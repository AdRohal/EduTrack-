Welcome to our site EduTrack

EduTrack is a comprehensive school management application designed to streamline and enhance the management of teachers, students, and classes within educational institutions. Supported by the GitHub Student Pack and Expertus.ma, EduTrack aims to improve the efficiency and quality of educational resource management, making it easier for administrators to oversee and optimize their educational environments.
